Cardiovascular

Tap each condition tile to expand practical primary-care guidance.

🩺 Hypertension

Open guidance
  • Confirm with home/ambulatory BP when possible.
  • Assess end-organ risk: renal function, diabetes, CVD history.
  • Lifestyle first + medication escalation per pathway.
  • Safety net for severe headache, neuro signs, chest pain.

💓 Atrial Fibrillation

Open guidance
  • Irregular pulse + ECG confirmation.
  • Stroke risk review (CHA₂DS₂-VASc) and bleeding risk.
  • Rate control and anticoagulation decisions by protocol.
  • Urgent escalation if haemodynamic instability.

💔 Heart Failure

Open guidance
  • Symptoms: orthopnoea, PND, oedema, exertional breathlessness.
  • Order BNP/NT-proBNP + U&E/LFT/FBC as baseline.
  • Check fluid status, medication tolerance, renal trends.
  • Escalate same-day if acute decompensation features.

⚡ Acute Coronary Syndrome

Open guidance
  • Central chest pain +/- radiation, nausea, diaphoresis.
  • Treat as time-critical emergency pathway.
  • Do not delay transfer for routine primary care workup.
  • Safety net atypical presentations in women/elderly/diabetes.

🦵 DVT / VTE suspicion

Open guidance
  • Apply pre-test probability score pathway.
  • Arrange D-dimer/imaging as appropriate by risk.
  • Identify provoking factors and bleeding considerations.
  • Escalate if PE symptoms emerge (SOB, pleuritic pain, syncope).

📉 Lipids & prevention

Open guidance
  • Use risk calculator + shared decision making.
  • Discuss non-HDL/LDL targets and adherence barriers.
  • Baseline LFT before statin; CK only if symptomatic.
  • Monitor response and side effects per pathway.

Use local and NICE guidance for exact thresholds, medicines, and referral criteria.
